## Frontier Airlines at a Glance

Frontier Airlines, designated by the IATA code F9, operates as a low-cost carrier based in the [United States](https://esim.techpawz.com/posts/esim-united-states/). With a route count of 30 and serving 12 airports, Frontier focuses on providing affordable travel options to a variety of destinations, primarily within the continental U.S.
