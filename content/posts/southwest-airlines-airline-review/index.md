## Southwest Airlines at a Glance

Southwest Airlines (IATA: WN) is a well-known low-cost carrier (LCC) based in the [United States](https://visafree.techpawz.com/posts/united-states-visa-free/). With a route count of 30, it serves a total of 14 airports, primarily focusing on domestic travel. The airline’s approach to pricing and service has made it a popular choice for budget-conscious travelers.

## Where Southwest Airlines Flies: Route Network

![southwest-airlines-airline-review](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/southwest-airlines-airline-review/body_2.jpg)


Southwest Airlines has established a diverse route network that connects key cities across the United States. Notable routes include Las Vegas to [Los Angeles](https://michelin.techpawz.com/posts/michelin-restaurants-los-angeles/), Chicago to [New York](https://michelin.techpawz.com/posts/michelin-restaurants-new-york/), and Denver to Phoenix. For instance, a flight from Los Angeles to Phoenix can range from $44 to $178, while a trip from Chicago to Las Vegas typically costs between $58 and $128. The airline also offers routes from major hubs like Atlanta to Chicago and Denver to Las Vegas, ensuring a wide range of travel options for passengers.
