## About Air Peace (P4)

Air Peace is a low-cost airline based in Nigeria, with a mission to provide affordable air travel options to its passengers. Established in 2013, Air Peace has quickly grown to become one of the largest carriers in West Africa. The airline’s focus on cost-efficiency allows it to offer competitive fares, making air travel accessible to a broader audience. While the airline’s primary operations are centered around domestic flights within Nigeria, it also serves a limited number of international destinations.

As a low-cost carrier, Air Peace operates under a business model that prioritizes affordability, often at the expense of some amenities typically found on full-service airlines. This means that while you might find lower ticket prices, additional services such as checked baggage and in-flight meals may come with extra fees. Understanding this model is key to navigating your travel experience with Air Peace.
