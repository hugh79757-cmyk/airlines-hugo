## What to Expect as a Low-Cost Carrier

![westjet-airline-review](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/westjet-airline-review/body_3.jpg)

As a low-cost carrier, Westjet is expected to adhere to several common practices that distinguish it from traditional full-service airlines. These practices often include:

- **Basic Fare Structure**: Travelers can expect lower base fares, but additional fees may apply for services such as checked baggage, seat selection, and in-flight meals. This allows customers to pay only for the services they choose to use.

- **No Frills**: Low-cost carriers typically provide a no-frills flying experience. This means that while passengers can expect a safe and efficient flight, amenities such as complimentary meals and drinks may not be included.

- **Point-to-Point Service**: Westjet may operate a point-to-point service model, which simplifies travel by connecting travelers directly between destinations without the need for layovers at hub airports.

- **Focus on Efficiency**: To maintain low fares, Westjet likely emphasizes operational efficiency, which can include quick turnaround times at airports and streamlined processes for boarding and disembarking.

- **Optional Add-Ons**: Passengers may have the option to purchase additional services, such as priority boarding or extra legroom, allowing them to customize their travel experience according to their preferences and budget.
